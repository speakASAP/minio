## Purpose
Store human-approved proposals to change protected MinIO intent documents.

## Rules
AI may draft proposals but must not approve them. Each amendment must list affected documents and downstream updates.

## Template
Use summary, reason, affected documents, impact, compatibility with original intent, and approval.
